About Me

Hey! I'm Amelia

I am a graphic designer from Canberra, just graduated year 12 at Dickson College. My journey in design combines my passions for visual storytelling, music, and creative problem-solving.

I specialise in graphic design, digital illustration , creative computing and media production. My work has been recognised with awards including 1st place in the CMET Light Rail Safety Week Competition (2025) and the Canberra Museum and Gallery Secondary School Art Prize (2023).

 My other passion is music,  through national choirs, musical theatre productions and vocal training.

📸 Follow me on Instagram @ameliabobbin_
