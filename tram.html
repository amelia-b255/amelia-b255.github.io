The Brief

Project Overview

Canberra Metro Operations (CMET) commissioned me to develop a visual communication campaign promoting light rail safety awareness.

My job was to create a visual campaign that educates and informs the target audience (15-24 years) about light rail safety. The designs needed to be both visually appealing and effective in communicating the safety message.
